Lucky's Lasagna

"I got ye' four basic food groups: Beans, Bacon, Whiskey, and Lard..."

This delicious Lucky's Lasagna is sure to warm you to the bone, increasing vitality and stamina

Ingredients:

  • Lucky's Secret Noodles
  • Lucky's Secret Sauce
  • Lucky's Secret Meat

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Boil Lucky's Secret Noodles for 10 minutes
  3. Fry Lucky's Secret Meat on both sides
  4. Stir in Lucky's Secret Sauce and combine other ingredients
  5. Enjoy
