Step One: Pick Blueberries...

Deb's Blueberry Pie:


Ingredients:

  • Pie Crust
  • 1 lb. Blue Berries
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • A little boy to help

Instructions:

  1. Set oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix blueberries and sugar
  3. Get a little boy to stir
  4. Pour into pie crust
  5. Bake for 45 minutes
  6. Let cool and enjoy!
